A Brief Backstory

Having grown up in both Los Angeles, CA and Corvallis, Oregon, I've been able to experience both the busy metropolitan life and quiet countryside living. Both locations hold a special place in my heart

I am student at Occidental College in Los Angeles, CA, where I am studying Diplomacy and World Affairs and East Asian Studies, with a minor in Japanese Studies. I am interested in policy making and striving to change the world through work with International Organizations or the US Foreign Service, and wish to obtain hands on experience helping those who most need it. You can find out more here.
